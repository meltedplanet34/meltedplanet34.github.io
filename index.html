    Do you like flowers?

    I certainly do

    At least since I've laid eyes on you


    The beauty of a rose

    Could never compare

    What lie under thy clothes


    Caught up in thine eyes

    More captivating than a camellia so rare

    None other could give me such a rise


    The thoughts no other woman could occupy

    U and I my favorite letters

    A hydrangea a marigold a daisy


    A sunflower

    That reminds me of you the most

    So that is my favorite flower
